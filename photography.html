About The Artist

Discover the story behind the lens

Hi, I'm Avhishek Shrestha

With over 8 years of professional photography experience, I specialize in capturing the essence of moments that tell compelling visual stories. My journey began with a simple point-and-shoot camera and has evolved into a lifelong passion for visual storytelling.

My work has been featured in several exhibitions and publications, and I've had the privilege of working with clients ranging from international brands to local businesses. What drives me is the ability to freeze time - to capture emotions, landscapes, and moments that would otherwise be lost to memory.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me teaching photography workshops or exploring new techniques to push the boundaries of my craft.
